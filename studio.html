Meet our studio

Luminous Pilates & Wellbeing Studio was created with the intention to not only offer top-notch Pilates sessions but also to promote serenity and relaxation for its patrons, where Pilates and interior design go hand in hand.

The studio includes two private rooms with top-of-the-line Stott Pilates equipment, as well as a larger room for group sessions. In addition to the equipment, this spacious room has a specially designed and custom-made shelf for storing all the necessary props for classes. Also, there is a reception area, changing room, bathroom with a shower for clients, kitchen, and a relaxation terrace.

Our co-founder, Andrea Solesa, and her team at Project A1 Design Hub designed the studio as a contemporary, tranquil, and light-filled space. The interior design is centered around a color scheme that features neutral, earthy, and pastel tones. They used a refined selection of materials, including oak wood, tactile textured wallpapers, and travertine slabs. The space reflects the Luminous Pilates brand and captures the concept of the practice with subtle details and fluid shapes that symbolize movement.

Lighting is of utmost importance, as the name Luminous suggests. It is thoughtfully selected to create a warm and inviting atmosphere, accentuating the goal of helping clients find their inner light. The design plays with the relationship between light and shadows, that is grounded in a deep connection and appreciation for nature. Our private rooms have wallpaper murals of tree branches, emphasizing this concept. The murals seamlessly blend with the high trees in front of our windows, creating the illusion of real shadows.

Every element of the space is intentional, down to the art pieces, as Andrea believes that clever design can influence the user experience. The hallway features a collection of books that provide an added element of wellness and self-discovery. Organic shaped mirrors, and beautifully designed doors are among the most notable elements, giving the space a sophisticated and elegant look. Additionally, the studio is filled with plants, which further enhance the calming and rejuvenating atmosphere.

The interior as a whole is influenced by nature. The bathroom mirror's wave-like design was inspired by the sea, while the spherical wall lamp symbolizes the sun. Sun as a symbol of light is represented as well with a special circular wall lighting fixture in one of the rooms, contributing to the overall feeling of warmth.

The combination of soothing colors, calming lighting, inspiring wall art, and natural elements all work together to promote relaxation and mindfulness. Join us for a class and experience the charm of this extraordinary setting for yourself.
