ONLINE STUDIO

Get ready for the ultimate at-home workout experience! We are excited to announce that we are currently developing an online app that will bring the LUMINOUS experience to you, wherever you are. Our app will offer high-quality virtual classes and a range of extra features that will help you accomplish your objectives from the comfort of your own home.

We understand that not everyone can join us in our physical studio, and that is why we are committed to expanding our reach and providing access to our classes and expertise to a wider audience. Our online app will offer the same level of excellence and attention to detail that you would expect from in-person classes, so you can be sure that you will be getting the best possible instruction and guidance.

To stay up-to-date with the release date of our online app and other relevant information, we encourage you to sign up for our newsletter.
