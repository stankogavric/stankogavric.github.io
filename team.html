We are LUMINOUS

Meet Andjela Filipovic, a highly qualified and dedicated certified Stott Pilates instructor and a co-founder of Luminous, who holds a Master's degree from the University of Novi Sad (Faculty of Sports and Physical Education) and Foro Italico University in Rome. With her extensive athletic background in gymnastics, volleyball, swimming, football, and athletics, and her broad education Andjela brings a deep understanding of anatomy and movement to her Pilates practice. Her commitment to continuous learning ensures that clients receive trainings programmed in line with the latest Pilates trends. Read more ...

Andjela's journey with Stott Pilates began when she discovered the unique combination of biomechanics, physical therapy, and conscious control of the body and movements that the practice offers. She was immediately drawn to the sense of strength, ease of movement, and satisfaction that she felt after her first session, and it was then that she knew she wanted to deepen her knowledge and experience with the method.

Her caring and approachable style make her a sought-after instructor and an ideal guide on your Pilates journey. She has a true gift for helping clients achieve their goals and inspiring them to reach their full potential. Andjela's expertise lies in a range of Pilates techniques and she has a special talent for adapting each session to suit the needs and physical abilities of her clients, ensuring that everyone feels comfortable and confident.

Let Andjela guide you and join her for a transformative Pilates experience that will leave you feeling stronger, more centered, and more connected to your body.

Andrea Soleša

Andrea Solesa, co-founder of Luminous Pilates Studio, is a distinguished interior designer with over a decade of professional experience. With her highly respected design studio, Project A1, she has made a name for herself as a leader in her field.

Her mental health challenges set her on a path of personal development, during which she discovered and fell in love with Stott Pilates. Today, as someone who has experienced firsthand the positive changes that this form of training can bring, she is dedicated to providing the best possible experience for our clients, by leveraging her vast expertise in entrepreneurship, marketing, interior design and product design. Read more ...

Not only did Andrea play a key role in crafting the branding story for Luminous, but she also designed the studio's interior, with a vision to create a warm and inviting atmosphere, full of soul, that would allow users to feel at ease. Her design philosophy is based on the belief that a well-designed space can have a positive impact on people's well-being, which is why she carefully selected each element of our interior to create a harmonious and uplifting environment that supports both physical and mental health.

But her work doesn't stop there. Andrea aims to reach wider audience through our social media pages, the online application we are developing and written resources on the website. In addition to Pilates related topics, these materials will cover proper nutrition, stress management and overall mental hygiene. By working on her own healing, Andrea has gained a sense of purpose and wants to openly address people, providing them with structure to gain stability and healthy habits.

Progress, as Andrea believes, cannot be achieved unless we confront our issues and actively listen to each other's experiences and perspectives.

You will enjoy his training sessions because he brings fun and laughter to each session, always seeking innovative ways to help and inspire his clients.

At Luminous studio, we believe that Pilates is suitable for everyone, and Milan's expertise in working with individuals of all ages and abilities makes him the ideal instructor for anyone looking to enhance their health and well-being through this method.
