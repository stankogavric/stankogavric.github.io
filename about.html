About Us

Welcome to Luminous Pilates & Wellbeing Studio (LMNS), a unique destination founded by Andjela Filipovic, a highly skilled and certified Stott Pilates instructor and Andrea Solesa, a renowned interior designer and Pilates enthusiast. They have joined forces to create a one-of-a-kind workout experience that combines professional instruction with stimulating surroundings. Andjela's professional expertise and Andrea's eye for aesthetics were instrumental in creating a space that is both calming and inspiring, where you can escape from the stresses of everyday life and focus on yourself.

While Stott Pilates is at the core of our approach at LMNS, we are also passionate about exploring movement in all its forms. We believe that movement is a powerful tool for self-expression, creativity, and personal growth, and we are always looking for new ways to challenge ourselves and our clients.

As a team, we value creative freedom and open-mindedness, and we are not bound by strict definitions or limitations. We approach each workout with a sense of playfulness and curiosity, experimenting with different techniques and modalities in order to create a unique style that is true to our own authentic nature. Our mission is to inspire and empower individuals to take control of their bodies, minds, and emotions through physical activity.

We understand that many people may feel insecure, inadequate, or intimidated by the idea of working out. Therefore, we strive to help these individuals overcome their fears and reclaim their power. By offering personalized attention and a private setting, Luminous Pilates & Wellbeing Studio provides a safe and supportive environment for people to work on themselves and reach their full potential.

We think that everyone should have access to a healthy and active lifestyle. Thus, we offer a variety of classes and sessions to suit all levels, from individual and duo sessions to dynamic or mat group classes. Our range of Stott Pilates equipment and props ensures that you have everything you need to get the most out of your workout.

At Luminous studio, we are committed to helping you shine, both inside and out. Whether you're a seasoned Pilates enthusiast or just starting out, our studio is the perfect place to rejuvenate your body and mind.

Join us on our mission to spread the transformative power of Stott Pilates and discover a healthier, happier you.
